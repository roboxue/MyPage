Ten most frequent topics around the world

As a part of my Northwestern University Text Analytics assignment, this visualization displayed the top ten topics around the world, based on all countries' wikipedia introduction page. All the vocabularies appeared in those introductions are classified into ten "topics", based on Latent_Dirichlet_allocation algorithm. The top ten words in each topic will be displayed as well.
